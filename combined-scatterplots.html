How temperature correlates with demographic features in Baltimore by neighborhood

by Adam Marton

Hotter neighborhoods tend to have higher rates of poverty, crime and unemployment and lower life expectancy. This doesn't mean, necessarily, that high temperatures cause these issues. McElderry Park is included in the data under the "Madison/East End" community statistical area. Click on a dot in the scatterplot to see the associated community and statistics.
